# How to Access Your Deployed Website

## On Railway Dashboard

1. Go to your Railway project dashboard (the page you're currently on)
2. Click on the **"alamin"** service/card
3. Look for a section called **"Domains"** or **"Networking"**
4. You'll see a URL like: `https://alamin-production.up.railway.app`
5. Click on that URL or copy it

## Alternative: Find the URL

1. In Railway dashboard, click on your **"alamin"** service
2. Go to the **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll down to **"Networking"** or **"Domains"** section
4. You'll see your public URL there

## Generate a Custom Domain (Optional)

1. In Railway, go to your service → **Settings** → **Networking**
2. Click **"Generate Domain"** or **"Add Domain"**
3. Railway will give you a URL like: `https://alamin-production.up.railway.app`
